STEP 1 — Find Your Network Info (on Windows)
Open CMD and run:
cmd- ipconfig
Note down:
Your Windows IP     →  e.g.  192.168.1.10
Your Router/Gateway →  e.g.  192.168.1.1
Your Subnet Mask    →  e.g.  255.255.255.0
